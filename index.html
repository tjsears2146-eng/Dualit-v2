Why Duality

Less filler.
More actual game.

Duality compresses constructed Magic into forty cards without shrinking the strategy. Every deck contains fifteen paired nonland cards, exactly ten lands, and no dead weight that wandered in because humans fear empty deck slots.

The result is a faster format where deck construction matters before the first draw and every duplicate can become fuel for Resonance.

Signature mechanic

Resonance

Once each turn, as you cast a spell, you may exile another card with the same name from your hand. If you do, you may spend mana as though it were mana of any color to cast that spell.

Resonance does not reduce the spell’s cost. It trades the second copy for perfect color fixing. You keep tempo, but you lose the twin forever. Convenient, cruel, and beautifully on theme.
